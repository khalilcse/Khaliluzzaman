## 🔬 Research Interests & Topics

My research focuses on leveraging computational frameworks to solve complex problems in intelligent systems, healthcare, HCI, and Digital Image Processing. Active areas of interest include:

* **Digital Image Processing & Computer Vision:** Developing robust algorithms for feature extraction, image enhancement, and structural analysis in complex visual data.
* **Deep Learning & Intelligent Systems:** Designing multi-layered neural networks and smart systems tailored for automated pattern recognition and decision-making.
* **Gait Recognition & Biometrics:** Investigating behavioral biometrics and motion-based recognition models to identify individuals through walking patterns.
* **Pattern Recognition:** Building classification paradigms and structural frameworks to analyze data sequences across varying domains.
* **Human-Computer Interaction (HCI):** Optimizing user-centric intelligent interfaces to improve interaction dynamics between humans and computing systems.
